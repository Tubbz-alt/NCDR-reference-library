About

The Metrics and Indicators Development Assurance and Advice Service (MIDAS) is the NHS England and NHS Improvement indicator governance service.

Its purpose is to apply standardised approaches to improve the management of metrics across analytical and information functions and to address the efficacy and quality of indicators in use. Data and analytical staff are encouraged to contact the MIDAS service desk when they wish to include existing indicators in new reports and dashboards or when they would like to develop, amend or retire indicators.

These metrics have been published on the reference library to enable those working in NHS England and NHS Improvement to use consistent and approved methodologies for their metrics to allow more accurate benchmarking across different areas and organisations. If you are interested in supporting MIDAS or have any questions, please contact the MIDAS service desk and we will get back to you.
